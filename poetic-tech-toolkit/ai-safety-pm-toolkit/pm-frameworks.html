product design: CIRCLES

CIRCLES Method
CComprehend — clarify the problem, user, constraints
IIdentify the customer — who are we solving for?
RReport customer needs — what are their pain points?
CCut through prioritization — which needs matter most?
LList solutions — brainstorm approaches
EEvaluate tradeoffs — pros/cons of each
SSummarize recommendation — your pick + reasoning

prioritization: RICE

RICE Scoring
FactorDefinition
ReachHow many users affected?
ImpactHow much value per user?
ConfidenceHow sure are you?
EffortPerson-months to build

Score = (Reach × Impact × Confidence) / Effort

stories: STAR-L

STAR-L for Behavioral Questions
SSituation — brief context
TTask — your specific responsibility
AAction — what YOU did (not the team)
RResult — quantified impact
LLearning — what you'd do differently
