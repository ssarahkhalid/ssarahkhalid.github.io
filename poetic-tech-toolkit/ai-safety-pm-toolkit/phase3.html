Phase 3: Research

weeks 18-26 · ~80 hours · from implementation to contribution

This phase transforms you from someone who understands AI safety to someone who can contribute to it. You'll build your own evaluation benchmark, read key papers deeply, and prepare for technical interviews.

week 18-19: evals foundations

why evals matter

Evaluations are the empirical foundation of AI safety. Every major safety org needs people who can design, build, and interpret evals.

goals

~15 hrs
  • Read UK AISI Inspect documentation
  • Run example evaluations
  • Understand Task, Solver, Scorer primitives

week 20-22: build your benchmark

benchmark ideas
  • Sycophancy detection across domains
  • Instruction-following edge cases
  • Value consistency under paraphrase
  • Reasoning faithfulness in CoT

week 23-24: deep paper reading

essential papers
  • Constitutional AI
  • Sleeper Agents
  • Weak-to-Strong Generalization
  • Alignment Faking

week 25-26: interview preparation

  • Finalize all behavioral stories (STAR format)
  • Practice technical explanations at all levels
  • Mock interviews with peers
  • Polish all portfolio artifacts
