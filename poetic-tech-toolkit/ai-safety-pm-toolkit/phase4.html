Phase 4: Emergence

weeks 27-34 · ~70 hours · networking sprint + applications

The technical preparation is complete. Now you emerge into the community — building relationships, submitting applications, and positioning yourself for the roles you've been preparing for.

the reality

70%+ of roles at top AI safety orgs are filled through referrals or inbound interest from known community members. Cold applications alone won't cut it.

target organizations

TierOrganizationsKey Roles
1Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepMindTrust & Safety PM, Evals PM
2UK AISI, US AISI, METR, ApolloEvaluations PM, Technical PM
3Redwood, Conjecture, CAISResearch PM, Program PM

week 27-28: network mapping

  • Research target organizations deeply
  • Create connection tracker spreadsheet
  • Draft outreach templates
  • Begin initial outreach (15 messages)

week 29-30: coffee chats

  • 5-8 coffee chats per week
  • Polish application materials
  • Tailor cover letters by organization

week 31-32: application sprint

  • Submit Tier 1 applications
  • Submit Tier 2 & 3 applications
  • Continue networking (3-4 chats/week)

week 33-34: interview mode

  • Mock interview sprint
  • Research interviewers
  • Prepare org-specific questions
