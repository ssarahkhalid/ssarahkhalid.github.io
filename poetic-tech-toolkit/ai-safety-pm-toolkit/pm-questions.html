Question Bank

prepare for these before your interviews

behavioral questions

background & motivation
  • Tell me about yourself (2-minute version)
  • Why AI safety? Why now?
  • Why this organization specifically?
  • How does your background prepare you for this role?
product management
  • Tell me about a product you shipped from concept to launch
  • How do you prioritize features?
  • Tell me about a time you had to cut scope
  • How do you handle disagreements with engineering?

technical questions

AI safety concepts
  • Why is AI alignment hard?
  • Explain RLHF and its limitations
  • What is the scalable oversight problem?
  • What's the difference between AI control and AI alignment?
  • What is mechanistic interpretability?

questions to ask them

  • What does success look like in the first 6 months?
  • What's the biggest challenge the team is facing?
  • How does this role interact with research teams?
  • How do you think about the balance between capabilities and safety?
