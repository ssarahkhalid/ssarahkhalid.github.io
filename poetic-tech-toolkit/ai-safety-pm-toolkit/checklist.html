Interview Readiness

track your progress and identify gaps before applying

technical safety — tier 1

Must know cold. Be able to explain at multiple levels of depth.

  • Explain transformers (4 articulation levels)
  • Explain attention mechanism (4 levels)
  • Explain RLHF and its limitations (4 levels)
  • Explain mechanistic interpretability (4 levels)
  • Explain scalable oversight challenge
  • Explain AI control vs alignment
  • Explain what makes a good eval
  • Have 3+ implementation debugging stories

technical safety — tier 2

  • Constitutional AI
  • Induction heads
  • Superposition
  • SAEs (conceptual)
  • UK AISI Inspect framework
  • Model-written evals
  • Responsible scaling policies
  • Emergent misalignment
  • Chain-of-thought monitoring

field landscape

  • Name the major safety teams at each big lab
  • Know public alignment agendas (RSP, Preparedness, FSF)
  • Discuss standing critiques of each lab's approach
  • Know key researchers in your areas of interest
  • Understand theory of change for 3-4 research agendas
