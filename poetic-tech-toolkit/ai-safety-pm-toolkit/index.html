AI Safety PM Toolkit

a 34-week curriculum for senior AI/ML product managers transitioning into AI safety roles at frontier labs

This toolkit bridges the gap between shipping production AI systems and contributing to alignment research. It's designed for experienced PMs who understand the product craft and want to develop the technical depth required for safety-focused roles at organizations like Anthropic, OpenAI, DeepMind, and emerging safety labs.

who this is for

Senior AI/ML product managers with 4+ years of experience shipping ML products who want to transition into technical PM roles at AI safety organizations. You understand product development deeply; now you need the safety-specific technical knowledge and community connections.
