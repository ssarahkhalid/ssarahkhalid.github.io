Welcome Edition January 2026

Introducing Mettadology

For the past ten months, I've been collecting. Links. Ideas. Patterns. Connections between the technical work of building AI systems and the inner work of training attention. Between the craft of hand-coded HTML and the discipline of meditation.

This collecting became a practice. The practice became three volumes. The volumes became Mettadology.

Now, four times a year, a new edition synthesizes what's emerging at the intersection of conscious technology and creative practice. Each one crafted with care. Slow synthesis for people building conscious technology.

About

Mettadology is created by Sarah Khalid, a Senior AI/ML Product Manager building machine learning systems in healthcare technology. The journal emerges from the intersection of technical practice and contemplative inquiry.

Mettadology began as a personal practice—ten months of collecting links, noting patterns, and tracing connections between technical work and inner development. The Founding Collection represents this initial exploration. Now, four times a year, Mettadology synthesizes what's emerging at the intersection of conscious technology and creative practice.

Part of Dialogos Labs—a creative technology studio focused on conscious AI development.
