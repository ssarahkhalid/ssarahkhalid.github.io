mettadology archives

curation across AI/ML, creative technology, philosophy, and practice

archival volumes of 2025 · curated by sarah khalid

this is an archived series of work that sits at the intersection of technical implementations and contemplative practice. links that made me think differently about alignment, embodiment, infrastructure, and community.

each volume follows a thread: starting with a question, weaving through research and practice, ending with synthesis. these aren't comprehensive surveys. they're curated perspectives on what it means to build technology consciously.

mettadology = methodology + metta (loving-kindness)
